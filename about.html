About Me

I’m a Computer Engineering student specializing in Cloud Computing, with a growing passion for building reliable, scalable, and meaningful technology. Whether it’s cloud systems, embedded devices, or full-stack development, I enjoy solving problems and understanding how things work behind the scenes. Currently, I’m expanding my skills in the semiconductor industry as a Reliability Product Testing Engineer intern at Infineon Technologies Singapore, where I support reliability testing and product performance analysis for high-quality semiconductor components.

My Story

Background

I was born and raised in Myanmar, where my early interest in technology began with curiosity about how devices, apps, and systems work. After moving to Singapore to pursue my Diploma in Computer Engineering at Singapore Polytechnic, I discovered a strong passion for both software and hardware—especially cloud systems, automation, and microcontroller projects. Each stage of my education has pushed me to explore new technologies and sharpen my problem-solving mindset.

What I'm Doing

Today, I’m interning at Infineon Technologies Singapore as a Reliability Product Testing Engineer. Outside of my internship, I actively build personal and school projects ranging from Raspberry Pi systems and STM32 microcontroller applications to full-stack web tools and cloud-based solutions.

Whether through cloud infrastructure, embedded systems, or long-term work in the semiconductor industry, my goal is to continue learning, innovating, and contributing to meaningful technological progress.
